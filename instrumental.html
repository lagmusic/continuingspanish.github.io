Instrumental

Music History

This course is taken in junior or senior year depending on the student's schedule. There are no requirements to qualify for the course, however it is a requirement to take the course.

This course culminates in 2 exams:
A NYSSMA exam and an exam provided by the school.
The NYSSMA exam is in January of senior year and is considered to be much easier than the LaGuardia exam.

AP Music Theory

This class is not required and can be taken in Sophomore, Junior, or Senior year. There is a test to qualify for it - this test is administered by the teacher of the class in the spring prior to the start of the class.

This class culminates in an AP exam in May.
